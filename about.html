About Me

Hi! I'm Sioh Ying Yi, a Year 2 Software Engineering student with a passion for building meaningful and user-friendly digital experiences. I enjoy turning ideas into code and I love exploring both the logic of backend systems and the creativity of frontend design.


Throughout my studies, I've gained hands-on experience with C++, Java and core web technologies including HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I'm especially interested in web development and UI/UX design, where I can combine technical skills with visual storytelling.


I'm always eager to learn, improve, and collaborate. Whether it’s a classroom project or a personal website, I enjoy the process of problem-solving and bringing ideas to life on screen.
